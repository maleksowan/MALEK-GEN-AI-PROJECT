University Student Information Knowledge Base

University Name:
Future Tech University (FTU)

General Information:
Future Tech University (FTU) is a private university that offers undergraduate programs in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, and Business. The academic year is divided into two main semesters: Fall and Spring, with an optional Summer semester.

Admissions:
Students can apply online through the official university admission portal. Required documents include a high school certificate, a national ID, and a personal photo. Admission decisions are usually announced within two weeks after submission.

Registration:
Course registration is completed online through the student portal during the announced registration period. Students are advised to complete registration on time, as late registration may result in additional fees.

Credit Hours:
Most undergraduate programs require the completion of 132 credit hours to graduate. Each course is typically worth 3 credit hours unless stated otherwise in the study plan.

Examinations:
Each course includes at least one midterm exam and one final exam. The final exam usually represents 40% of the total course grade. Students must bring their university ID to all examinations.

Grading System:
Grades are calculated based on assignments, quizzes, midterm exams, and final exams. The grading scale ranges from A (Excellent) to F (Fail). A minimum grade of D is required to pass a course.

IT Services:
All students receive a university email account and access to the eLearning system. Password or technical issues can be resolved by contacting the IT Helpdesk.

eLearning System:
The eLearning platform is used to upload lecture materials, assignments, and important announcements. Students are responsible for checking the system regularly.

Academic Support:
The university provides academic advising and tutoring services. Students can book appointments through the Student Services Office.

Graduation Requirements:
To graduate, students must complete all required courses, maintain a minimum cumulative GPA of 2.0, and clear all financial obligations.
